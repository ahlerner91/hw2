About Us

This is Alison Lerner, our CEO and founder of Skincare by Alison. She proudly hails from Los Angeles and is currently receiving her MBA degree from the Kellogg School of Managment at Northwestern University.

Our Story

We are a female founded and run startup that aims to revolutionalize the skincare market. Alison was tired of paying a premium for top of the line skincare. She founded Skincare by Alison to cut out the middlemen and create prestige products for customers like you.

All products are sustainably sourced and not tested on animals (except bugs).
