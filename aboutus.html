About Our Founder

This is Alison Lerner, our CEO and founder of Skincare by Alison.

Our Story

Alison was tired of paying a premium for top of the line skincare. She founded Skincare by Alison to cut out the middlemen and create prestige products for customers like you.

All products are sustainably sourced and not tested on animals (except bugs).
